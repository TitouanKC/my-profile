Numbers

Numbers are one of my passions. Indeed, during my childhood I played many board games such as Monopoly, and I could spend hours doing calculations to determine the best strategies to adopt! Then, in high school, I continued to play with numbers by taking the mathematics and physical sciences specialties and the expert maths option.

Videogames

When you look at me, you may not realize that I can spend my whole day playing strategy games, and more particularly historical video games. For me, they allow me to escape my daily routine and travel back in time. Among these, I'm particularly fond of Creative Assembly's Total War franchise and Paradoxe Interactive's games.

Technology

As I love video games, I need a computer powerful enough to run them. So I need to know how to choose the components for my computer, and like all students I'm broke, I need to keep abreast of the latest innovations and sales. That's how I can spend hours reading articles on technology, and I have to say that all these technical advances fascinate me!

Politics

My grandmother is a history and geography teacher, and it was she who gave me my passion for history and geopolitics. As a result, I keep up to date with French and international news, particularly to understand the origins of conflicts. Like, I don't know, the Russo-Ukrainian and Israeli-Palestinian conflicts!
